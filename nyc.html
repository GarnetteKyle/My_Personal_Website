New York City (New York, USA)

As a Filipino who grew up watching American films, New York City is one of the cities that is often featured in movies. Seeing its towering skyline and fast paced lifestyle on screen made me curious about what it would be like in real life. I am especially excited to visit Times Square, which appears in many iconic scenes with its dazzling lights and energetic atmosphere. Experiencing the city in person would feel like stepping into the movies I watched growing up while also discovering its culture and landmarks for myself.
