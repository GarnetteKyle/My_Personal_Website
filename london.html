London (England, UK)

When I was a kid, my neighbors would often call me Mr.Bean, because it is my favorite thing to watch (Both animated and live action episodes). Also when I was a kid, I was very clumsy. I always tend to destroy or hurt myself accidentally, which is the Mr.Bean character. Because of this, I grew up wanting to visit London, where many of the scenes took place. Aside from the nostalgia, I am also fascinated by the city’s infrastructure, such as its historic buildings, roads, and transportation systems that I often saw in the show. Visiting London would feel like stepping into my childhood while also appreciating the real life environment behind it.
