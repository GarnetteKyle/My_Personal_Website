My Favorite Movies

Genre: Comedy / Drama

3 Idiots

Director: Rajkumar Hirani
Main Cast: Aamir Khan, R. Madhavan, Sharman Joshi
Released: 2009
Rating: 8.4/10 IMDb
A heartwarming and inspiring story that explores the pressures of the Indian education system through the lives of three engineering students. The film follows their friendship, struggles with academic expectations, and the pursuit of passion over rote learning. It highlights the importance of creativity, empathy, and thinking beyond conventional success.

Genre: Drama / Family

Like Stars On Earth (Taar Zameen Par)

Director: Aamir Khan
Main Cast: Darsheel Safary, Aamir Khan
Released: 2007
Rating: 8.4/10 IMDb
A moving drama about Ishaan, a young boy struggling with dyslexia, whose unique talents are overlooked by the school system. When a compassionate art teacher recognizes his potential, Ishaan discovers confidence, creativity, and the joy of learning. The film sensitively portrays the challenges of learning disorders and the transformative power of encouragement and understanding.

Genre: Romance / Drama

On Your Wedding Day (Korean Version)

Director: Lee Seok-geun
Main Cast: Park Bo-young, Kim Young-kwang
Released: 2018
Rating: 7.7/10 IMDb
A nostalgic and emotional romantic drama that traces the bittersweet journey of first love. Seung-hee and Woo-yeon meet in school, sharing intimate and awkward moments that linger throughout their lives. The story captures the nuances of growing up, personal growth, and the impact of choices on relationships, blending humor, heartbreak, and gentle nostalgia.

Genre: Drama

Dead Poets Society

Director: Peter Weir
Main Cast: Robin Williams, Robert Sean Leonard, Ethan Hawke
Released: 1989
Rating: 8.1/10 IMDb
Set in an elite all-boys preparatory school, the film follows an unorthodox English teacher, John Keating, who inspires his students to embrace individuality, seize the day, and pursue their true passions. The story challenges conformity, explores friendship and rebellion, and highlights the power of literature and mentorship to shape young lives.
