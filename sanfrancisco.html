San Francisco (California, USA)

As a Filipino who grew up watching American Films, San Francisco is one of the cities that is often featured in movies. Also, as a kid who plays GTA San Andreas, playing in the city that is based from San Francisco, makes me wonder what it feels to be there. Would I feel what I felt when I was a kid? One of the Iconic Things that I remember every time I hear San Francisco is their Cable Car System, which I saw in GTA San Andreas. I remember when I was playing it as a kid, I always tried to chase it and get on top of it, I would often use cheats to do so. I hope I will be able to do it in the future, not just in my dreams as a kid.
