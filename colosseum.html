Colosseum (Rome, ITALY)

I want to visit the Colosseum because it is one of the greatest architectural and historical achievements of ancient Rome. After watching Gladiator, I became more interested in the real history behind the arena, where gladiators once fought and large public spectacles were held for thousands of spectators. Seeing it in person would allow me to better understand Roman culture, engineering, and the significance of the events that took place there. Visiting the Colosseum would be a meaningful way to experience history beyond what I have learned from films.
